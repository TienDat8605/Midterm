# Mission: Add Story NPC Dialogue to the Unity Game

## Why
Place story characters throughout the climbing maps so they can deliver readable story moments to each locally controlled slime.

## Success looks like
- NPC sprites can be positioned visually in a gameplay scene.
- Father automatically presents the opening objective after the local slime spawns.
- Dialogue types progressively in a world-space speech bubble and remains local to each Photon client.

## Constraints
- Unity 6000.3 with the Input System and existing Photon/PUN player ownership.
- Keep the first version small and Inspector-driven.

## Out of scope
- Branching choices, quest state, localization, voice acting, and synchronized multiplayer dialogue.
